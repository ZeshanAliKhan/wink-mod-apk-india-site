# Content Style Guide

Use this guide whenever you edit or expand content for `Wink Mod Apk India Hub`.

## Core Rule

Write like a real human with direct, hands-on experience in wink mod apk. The page should feel useful fast, not padded.

## Mandatory Writing Rules

- Use very clear, simple language that an 8th-grade reader can follow.
- Use active voice only.
- Keep sentences short, but make each sentence carry useful information.
- Give the main answer early.
- Stay tightly focused on the exact topic of the page.
- Avoid repeating the same keyword, product name, or entity again and again.
- Use natural word variation and semantically related terms so the topic stays clear.
- Keep paragraphs, bullets, steps, and FAQ blocks in the format that best fits the information.
- Compress wording, not facts. Do not remove important details just to make text shorter.

## Strictly Avoid

- AI phrases
- robotic transitions
- filler text
- hype
- exaggeration
- bluff
- empty storytelling
- vague general advice
- long setup before the real answer
- keyword stuffing

## Editing Rules For Existing Generated Articles

- Keep one clear H1 only.
- Keep the intro direct and useful within the first paragraph.
- Keep the FAQ practical and specific.
- Keep numbered steps for how-to content.
- Keep comparison points balanced and plain.
- Keep examples realistic and short.
- Keep conclusions short, useful, and action-focused.

## SEO-Safe Content Rules

- Do not delete canonical tags, JSON-LD, meta tags, or sitemap files by hand.
- Do not force the exact keyword into every sentence.
- Use related terms in headings and body text where they fit naturally.
- Keep internal links useful and relevant to the sentence they appear in.
- Use external links only where a citation, definition, tool source, or data source helps the reader.

## Final Test Before Publishing

- Does the first paragraph answer the main intent quickly?
- Does the article sound like a person who knows the topic?
- Can a beginner understand it without extra explanation?
- Is there any repeated phrase that sounds templated?
- Can any sentence be shortened without losing a fact?
